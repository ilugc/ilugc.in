### Talk 1

- Narenderan started his talk about `Learning linux on Android`
- Explained about various way you can reach a terminal in android (`adb shell` or `termux`)
- Showed how to use `adb shell` to connect your android device from your laptop
- Showed how to install `termux` and start a terminal
- Showed how to use `andronix` to create a full fledged `xfce desktop environment` in `android`
- Showed how to use `VNC` to connect to the desktop environment running in android from the laptop

### Talk 2
