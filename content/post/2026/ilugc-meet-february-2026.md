Indian Linux Users Group, Chennai [ ILUGC ] has been spreading awareness on Free/Open Source Software (F/OSS) in Chennai since January 1998.

For the month of February 2026, we shall meet Physically at Indian Institute Of Technology Madras (IITM), Aerospace Engineering. Registered participants through https://register.ilugc.in arriving at IITM gates need to tell that you are there to attend "ILUGC Monthly Meet" so that IITM security will verify your details and allow you to get in.

### Talk 2

**Topic**: Landlock LSM\
**Description**: Landlock is a Linux Security Module that allows\
unprivileged applications to set access restrictions for itself, which
are then enforced by Landlock, to sandbox and increase the security of
applications.\
**Duration**: 30 minutes\
**Full Name**: Abdun Nihaal\
**About Speaker**: Graduate student
