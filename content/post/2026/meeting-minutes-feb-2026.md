Here is the quick recap of what happend in ILUGC monthly meet, February 14, 2026


### Intro 

- Gopi welcomed the participants and gave a small introduction about ILUGC 


### Talk 0 

- Mohan talked about WebAuthn 
- First explained asymmetric encryption and how it functions as the cornerstone of modern communication 
- Enlisted various openssl commands that lets you pack public keys in certificates 
- Introduced WebAuthn as an asymmetric passwordless authentication 
- He demonstrated the usage of ubi key which can be used in WebAuthn 
- Concluded the talk by taking in few questions about encryption and authentication
