Here is the quick recap of what happend in Ilugc Monthly meet, Dec 13, 2025.

### Intro

- Mohan welcomed participants

### Talk 0

- Gopinathan started explaining about his experience debugging a `broken window environment` issue
- During `switching` from `KDE` to `Gnome`, his packages screwed up and ended up `without any Graphical Environment`
- The switch triggered `segfault whenever Xorg starts`
- With help from mohan, Gopinathan installed `official nvidia drivers properly`
- Issue got fixed

![talk0](/images/20251213-talk0.jpeg)

### Talk 1

- Irfan started his speech about `IRC` and `Tilde` communities
- Explained what IRC means, `history of IRC`, how it works
- Briefed about how to `join IRC server`, how to `discover channels`, how to `join a particular channel` etc.
- Explained about `helpful Bots` and `Harmful Bots` inside IRC network
- Introduced `Tilde communities` around the world, why `they hate javascript` etc.
- Deep dived into `Hacker culture`, what really means `social computing` and `what FOSS achieved` in this space.

![talk0](/images/20251213-talk1.jpeg)

### Talk 2

- Sakthi started his speech about `Talos Linux`
- Gave overview about `Kubernetes clusters` how we can use our own computer to create `minimal Kubernates cluster`.
- Introduced Talos, explained how Talos differs from other `lightweight Kubernetes distros`.
- Explained how `Talos boots`, how `everything is API driven`, the significance of `talosctl`
- Demonstrated how to `install Talos` and `boostrap` it.
- As a general guidence, suggested few books to `get started with Linux` to participants.

### After talks discussion

- Participants introduced themselves
- Mohan provided popular FOSS news from the last month
- Participants had lively discussion about `Fundamentals of Learning`
- Participants discussed about `Custom ROMs and difficulties`.
- Participants discussed about `Running linux on Mac` (Mac with x86 as well as Mac with Apple Silicon), talked about `AashiLinux` efforts to port Linux to Mac with Apple Silicon
- Participants discussed about the importance of `Stable and LTS Distros` as well as benifits of `Rolling Distros`
- Took group photo
- Windup
