### Intro 

- Gopi welcomed the participants and gave a small introduction about ILUGC 

### Talk 0 

- Irfan started the talk with the history of operating systems 
- He introduced how ttys were the earliest interactive i/o systems 
- Explained meaning of terminals and how modern terminal emulators are different 
- Distinguished between shells and terminals 
- Demonstrated the linux command structure along with shell environments 
- He also explained how processes are forked in linux

### Talk 1 

- Syed starting his talk by explaing the nature of ctfs 
- He explained the logical steps involved in sovling ctfs

### Talk 2 

- Madhav wanted to give a brief talk about the Linux xz backdoor
