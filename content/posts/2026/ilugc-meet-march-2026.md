awareness on Free/Open Source Software (F/OSS) in Chennai since
January 1998.

Institute Of Technology Madras (IITM), Aerospace Engineering.
Registered participants through https://register.ilugc.in arriving at
IITM gates need to tell that you are there to attend "ILUGC Monthly
Meet" so that IITM security will verify your details and allow you to
get in.

### Talk 0

**Topic:** Website domain expiry monitor with Prometheus, grafana and python\
**Description:** I lost many websites as I forget to renew the domains on time. 
As a fix, built a system with python, Prometheus and grafana to show the expiry
of all the domains as a dashboard.\
**Duration:** 30 minutes\
**Full Name:** Shrinivasan T\

### Talk 2

**Topic:** Choicejacking Attacks\
**Description:** Choicejacking attacks are USB based attacks primarily
targeting Android phones, that allows malicious mobile chargers to exploit
our phones. In this talk, we will discuss USB attacks, how Choicejacking
attacks works and how we can protect ourselves from them.\
**Duration:** 30-45 mins\
**Full Name:** Abdun Nihaal\
**About Speaker:** Computer science graduate student

The talks will be followed by Q&A and discussions.
